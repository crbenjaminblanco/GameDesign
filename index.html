About Us

We are a team of computer science students from the University of Costa Rica, who tries to combine the fun and awareness of what is happening around the world through video games.

Polar Bear

Polar Bear is a game where we have to help a polar bear find his home, an igloo, before all the ice in his path melts, if this happens he will be lost in desertic ocean. In his adventure he will meet allies, that will help him to find his home before a catastrophic thing happens. But he will also meet enemies who want to hunt the poor bear, and the way to defeat them is through his friends (The penguins).
